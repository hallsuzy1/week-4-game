Welcome to The Recess Game!

Directions!

You will be given a random Magic Number at the start of the game.
There are four Recess characters below. By clicking on each character you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
You win the game by matching your total score to the Magic Number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the Magic Number.
The value of each character is hidden from you until you click on it.
Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of the Magic Number and of each Character.
